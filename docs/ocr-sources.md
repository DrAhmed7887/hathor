# Pediatric Vaccination Card Image Data Sources (LMIC)

We located several relevant image datasets and studies. The key finds are summarized below with links, dataset names, and access notes. When raw images are not openly available, we list contact authors (with emails if found) from recent papers to request data. All citations below reference the sources we found.  

- **Kaggle “Vaccination-Cards-Images-Dataset”** (open access via signup). This public Kaggle dataset (by M. Talha Khan) contains cellphone photographs of vaccination cards. It covers general childhood immunization cards (likely multi-country). *Access*: Free Kaggle download (account signup). (Direct link via Kaggle: [Vaccination-Cards-Images-Dataset on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mtalhakhan/vaccination-cards-images-dataset) – describes images of vaccination cards.【71†L0-L1】.)  
  *Access requirement*: Kaggle account (no paywall).

- **DRC 2022 VCS Home-Based Record (HBR) Photos** (referenced). A large dataset of **photos of immunization cards** was collected in the Democratic Republic of the Congo 2022 Vaccine Coverage Survey. This is described in Kibeti *et al.* (Vaccine 2025). The paper analyzed ~2,768 card photos【52†L78-L87】. *Data link*: Not published; availability “on request”【52†L119-L123】. *Contact*: Corresponding authors Dorcas M. Kibeti (Kinshasa School of Public Health) and Nicole A. Hoff (UCLA; nhoff84@ucla.edu【66†L121-L123】) – Hoff is UCLA-DRC director who co-supervised the study. (No dataset link; cite: Kibeti *et al.*, Vaccine 59:127248 (2025)【52†L78-L87】.)

- **The Gambia 2022 Vaccine Card Images**. Burstein *et al.* (medRxiv 2025) describe 349 photographed Gambian child vaccination cards (curated to 335) used for OCR research【21†L81-L90】【19†L31-L39】. They mention a curated image dataset of HBRs. *Data link*: Not provided in paper (medRxiv preprint). *Contact*: Roy Burstein (Gates Foundation/IDM) and Alieu Sowe (Gambia Ministry of Health) – likely corresponding. Burstein’s work email can be found via Gates/IDM websites (or see DOI above). (Cite: Burstein *et al.* medRxiv 2025【21†L81-L90】.)

- **IIT-Delhi “MCP Card” Dataset (India)**. Pant *et al.* (ACM J. Comput. Sustain. Soc. 2023) collected **5,388 “Mother and Child Protection” card images** in India【78†L430-L438】. They state *“We will publicly release this dataset”* of 69,800 segmented date regions【78†L430-L438】. (Likely to be available via project/GitHub; the paper’s authors are at IIT-Delhi/Gram Vaani.) *Data link*: Not found publicly yet. *Contact*: Aaditeshwar Seth (IIT Delhi, as corresponding author) or Devesh Pant (authors on paper). Emails (e.g., aseth@cse.iitd.ac.in【30†L79-L83】) can be used. (Cite: Pant *et al.*, 2023【30†L67-L75】【78†L430-L438】.)

- **UCLA-DRC Vaccination Coverage Surveys (multiple)**. Beyond the DRC 2022 VCS (above), the UCLA-DRC collaboration has conducted successive vaccination coverage surveys with HBR photos (Kibeti *et al.*, Vaccine 2025; other public health surveillance). No open image repo; contact Dr. Nicole Hoff (UCLA, email above) or Dorcas Kibeti for data requests.

- **Zenodo/Dryad/Harvard Dataverse Searches**: No dedicated open child-vaccination card image collections were found on Zenodo or Dryad. We did not find publicly deposited pediatric immunization card photo datasets in these repositories.

- **Other leads**:  
  - Burkina Faso, Ethiopia, etc.: We found no open image sets from DHS or WHO. DHS surveys generally record immunization via paper forms, but raw card images are not public.  
  - PhysioNet: No relevant image bank found.  
  - USAID/DHS: No publicly linked image data for vaccination cards.  

## Recent Papers (Dataset NOT linked) – Contact Info

From our search, the **three most relevant recent papers (2023–2025)** without explicit datasets are:

1. **Kibeti *et al.*, *Vaccine* (2025)** – DRC vaccine coverage survey, HBR photos (as above). *Corresponding authors*: Dorcas M. Kibeti and Nicole A. Hoff (UCLA; **nhoff84@ucla.edu**【66†L121-L123】).

2. **Burstein *et al.*, *medRxiv* (2025)** – Gambia HBR image transcription. *Authors*: Roy Burstein (Gates Foundation/IDM) and Alieu Sowe (Gambia MOH). Contact via institutional emails (e.g., Roy Burstein at Gates Foundation email or IDM).

3. **Pant *et al.*, *ACM J. Comput. Sustain. Soc.* (2023)** – India MCP card OCR dataset. *Corresponding*: Aaditeshwar Seth (IIT-Delhi; aseth@cse.iitd.ac.in【30†L79-L83】).

Each of these can be contacted for raw images. Their papers confirm the existence of image collections (HBR photos).
